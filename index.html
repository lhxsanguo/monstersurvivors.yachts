About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors challenges you to survive wave after wave of monstrous enemies. Collect powerful weapons, upgrade your skills, and master unique abilities to stay alive as long as possible.

This fast-paced game offers endless replayability with procedurally generated levels, increasing difficulty, and a variety of monsters with unique attack patterns. Can you become the ultimate survivor?

Key Features

  • Fast-paced monster survival action
  • Procedurally generated waves and levels
  • Wide variety of weapons and upgrades
  • Challenging boss battles
  • Beautiful modern graphics powered by Unity
